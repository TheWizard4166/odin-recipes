Healthier Homemade Mac & Cheese

To make this rich mac and cheese recipe healthier we use whole wheat bread crumbs,
low-fat milk and cheese, whole wheat noodles, and reduced the amount of butter.
Serve with a salad for a healthy meatless dinner.

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) package of whole wheat macaroni (such as Smart Taste)
  • 2 tablespoons of butter
  • 2 and a half tablespoons of all purpose flour
  • 2 cups of shredded low fat cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 a cup of grated parmesan cheese
  • 3 cups of low fat(1%) milk
  • 2 tablespoons of butter
  • 1/2 cup whole wheat bread crumbs
  • 1 pinch of paprika

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Bring a large
    pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook elbow macaroni in
    boiling water, stirring occasionally until cooked through but
    firm to the bite, 8 minutes. Drain.
  2. Melt 2 tablespoons of butter in a saucepan over medium heat. Stir
    in flour to make a roux. Slowly add milk to the roux, stirring
    constantly. Stir in cheddar and Parmesan cheeses and cook over low
    heat until the cheese is melted and the sauce is thic, for about 3 minutes.
    Place macaroni in large baking dish and pour sauce over macaroni. Stir well
  3. Melt 2 tablespoons of butter in a skillet over medium heat. Add breadcrumbs
    and stir until butter is absorbed, for 2 to 3 minutes. Spread over the macaroni
    to cover. Sprinkle with paprika.
  4. Bake in preheated oven until the cheese sauce is hot and the breadcrumbs are
    browned, for about 30 minutes.
